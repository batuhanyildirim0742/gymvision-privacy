Privacy Policy for GymVision

Effective Date: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GymVision ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use the GymVision mobile application (the "App").

1. What the App Does

GymVision is a fitness and exercise guidance application. It provides general exercise information, an exercise library, and an AI-assisted camera feature ("AI Camera") that analyzes images of gym equipment or exercises to help you identify them.

Health & Fitness Disclaimer: GymVision provides general informational content only. The App is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or rehabilitation coaching, and does not promise or guarantee injury prevention or fitness results. Always consult a qualified healthcare or fitness professional before starting any exercise program.

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

We minimize the data we collect. When you use the App, we process the following information:

A. Images (AI Camera)

If you choose to use the AI Camera feature, you may take a photo or select an image from your device's gallery.

  • Processing: The selected image is securely transmitted to our backend servers and processed using cloud-based artificial intelligence (Google Gemini) solely for the purpose of recognizing the gym equipment or exercise.
  • Retention: We do not intentionally store, save, or use these raw images for any other purpose. They are processed ephemerally (transiently) to generate recognition results and are then discarded.

B. Anonymous Device Identifiers

To prevent abuse, secure our backend services, and manage usage quotas, we use Firebase services which collect certain anonymous identifiers:

  • Firebase Anonymous Authentication: When you use the App, an anonymous unique identifier (UID) is generated for your device. This UID is completely anonymous and is not linked to your name, email, or identity.
  • Quota and Cooldown: We store your anonymous UID temporarily in our database (Firestore) alongside a numerical counter to enforce daily usage limits and prevent spam requests to our AI services.
  • App Check & Play Integrity: We use Google Play Integrity and Firebase App Check to verify that requests are coming from an unmodified, official version of GymVision. This helps prevent fraud and secures our infrastructure.

3. Data Sharing and Service Providers

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted service providers necessary to operate the App's core functionality:

  • Google Cloud & Firebase: We use Google's infrastructure to host our backend, manage anonymous authentication, and enforce security policies.
  • Google Gemini API: We transmit your AI Camera images to Google Gemini strictly to perform the image recognition functionality.

These providers process data according to their own strict security standards and privacy policies.

4. Advertising and Analytics

The App currently does not display advertisements, nor does it embed third-party advertising tracking SDKs. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

5. User Choices and Data Deletion

Because the App does not require user accounts (email/password) and operates using an anonymous device ID, we do not possess identifiable information to delete upon request. However, if you wish to clear the anonymous ID and local data, you may uninstall the App or clear the App's data from your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption in transit, to protect the transmission of your data (such as images) between your device and our servers.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and, where required by law, providing additional notice.

GymVision, bir fitness ve egzersiz rehberi uygulamasıdır. Genel egzersiz bilgileri, bir egzersiz kütüphanesi ve spor ekipmanlarını veya egzersizleri tanımlamanıza yardımcı olmak için görselleri analiz eden yapay zeka destekli bir kamera özelliği ("AI Camera") sunar.

Herhangi bir egzersiz programına başlamadan önce daima uzman bir sağlık veya fitness profesyoneline danışın.

Topladığımız verileri en aza indiriyoruz. Uygulamayı kullandığınızda aşağıdaki bilgileri işliyoruz:

A. Görseller (AI Camera)

AI Camera özelliğini kullanmayı seçerseniz, bir fotoğraf çekebilir veya cihazınızın galerisinden bir görsel seçebilirsiniz.

  • İşleme: Seçilen görsel güvenli bir şekilde sunucularımıza iletilir ve yalnızca söz konusu spor ekipmanını veya egzersizini tanımak (egzersiz tanıma) amacıyla bulut tabanlı yapay zeka (Google Gemini) kullanılarak işlenir.

Kötüye kullanımı önlemek, arka uç hizmetlerimizi korumak ve kullanım kotalarını yönetmek için belirli anonim tanımlayıcılar toplayan Firebase hizmetlerini kullanıyoruz:

  • Firebase Anonymous Authentication: Uygulamayı kullandığınızda, cihazınız için anonim ve benzersiz bir kimlik (UID) oluşturulur. Bu UID tamamen anonimdir; isminiz, e-posta adresiniz veya kişisel kimliğiniz ile bağlantılı değildir.

  • Firebase App Check / Play Integrity: İsteklerin GymVision'ın değiştirilmemiş, resmi bir sürümünden geldiğini doğrulamak için Google Play Integrity ve Firebase App Check kullanıyoruz. Bu, dolandırıcılığı önlemeye ve altyapımızı güvence altına almaya yardımcı olur.
